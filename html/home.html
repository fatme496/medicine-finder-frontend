About VitaMed

VitaMed is a modern humanitarian platform that solves two challenges: people in urgent need of medications, and the waste of unused medicines.

We offer: 1. A smart search tool that connects users to pharmacies nearby that have the medicine in stock.
2. A donation system where users can give away valid, unused medicines.

The goal is simple: make medications more accessible, reduce waste, and encourage community support.
